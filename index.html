Raccoon Olympics

The greatest trash-panda competition on Earth

Welcome to the Raccoon Olympics!

Join us for a life-changing event where raccoons compete in incredible challenges like Trash Dash, Fence Climbing, and Snack Steal. Fun for the whole family!

Trash Dash

Competitors race through obstacle courses made of trash cans and alleyways.

Fence Climbing

Competitors must climb three different obstacles of great height.

Snack Steal

Competitors must grab the most snacks from trash cans and garbage cans in 5 minutes.
